How can we help?

Find answers to frequently asked questions about our loyalty programme.

You earn EatPoints every time you make a purchase at participating restaurants using your linked card or app. Typically, you earn 1 point for every $1 spent. Keep an eye out for double point events!
You can redeem your points directly through the "Redeem Rewards" section of the app. Rewards include discounts, free items, and exclusive access to events at our partner locations.
EatPoints are valid for 12 months from the date they were earned. We will send you a reminder email 30 days before any points are due to expire.
Navigate to the "My Card" section and click the "Add Card" button. You can securely scan your card using your camera or enter the details manually.
